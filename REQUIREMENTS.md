# EcoSphere ESG Management Platform: System Requirements

## 1. Project Overview
EcoSphere aims to integrate Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) metrics directly into day-to-day ERP operations[cite: 1]. The platform will measure sustainability metrics, encourage employee participation, and provide meaningful reports for management[cite: 1].

## 2. Core Modules
The platform is divided into four main modules[cite: 1]:
*   **Environmental:** Carbon accounting, emission factors, sustainability goals, and carbon reports[cite: 1].
*   **Social:** CSR activities, employee participation, diversity metrics, and engagement tracking[cite: 1].
*   **Governance:** ESG policies, audits, compliance tracking, and governance reports[cite: 1].
*   **Gamification:** Challenges, badges, XP, rewards, and leaderboards to drive engagement[cite: 1].

## 3. Core Business Rules (Strict Compliance)
The system must enforce the following automated rules[cite: 1]:
*   **Auto Emission Calculation:** Carbon transactions must be calculated automatically from operational records (Purchase, Manufacturing, Expenses, Fleet) using relevant Emission Factors[cite: 1].
*   **Evidence Requirement:** CSR Activity participation cannot be approved without an attached proof file[cite: 1].
*   **Badge Auto-Award:** Badges must be automatically assigned when an employee's XP or completed challenge count satisfies the specific unlock rule[cite: 1].
*   **Reward Redemption:** Employees can redeem XP/Points for rewards (subject to stock availability), which deducts from their balance[cite: 1].
*   **Compliance Ownership:** Every compliance issue requires an assigned Owner and Due Date; overdue open issues must trigger flags[cite: 1].
*   **Notification System:** Alerts must be sent for new compliance issues, CSR/Challenge approvals, policy reminders, and badge unlocks[cite: 1].

## 4. Reporting Requirements
The system must generate the following reports[cite: 1]:
*   Environmental Report[cite: 1]
*   Social Report[cite: 1]
*   Governance Report[cite: 1]
*   ESG Summary Report[cite: 1]
*   Custom Report Builder (exportable to PDF/Excel/CSV) with filtering by Department, Date Range, Module, Employee, Challenge, and ESG Category[cite: 1].
